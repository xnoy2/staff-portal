Getting started

Logging in

Sign in to reach your portal. Your manager creates your account and gives you a temporary password.

  1. Open the portal in your browser: staff.bespokegardenroomsballycastle.co.uk.
  2. Enter your email and password, then press Log in.
  3. If it's your first time, you'll be asked to set your own password — choose a strong one and confirm it.
  4. You'll land on your Dashboard.
Tip Forgot your password or can't get in? Contact your manager — they can reset it for you.

Your home base

The Dashboard

The first page after logging in. It's your daily hub — clock in/out, see your hours, leave, jobs, and today's log at a glance.

What's on the page

  • The Clock — your live clock-in status and the big Clock In / Clock Out button.
  • My QR Code — present to a site head to be scanned in/out on site.
  • Today's Log — a shortcut to write your end-of-day summary.
  • This Week's Hours — a chart of hours worked each day.
  • Annual Leave — days remaining, with a link to book time off.
  • Upcoming Jobs — jobs assigned to you for the coming days.
  • Recent Entries — your latest clock records and their status.
Navigation The menu on the left opens every page. On a phone, tap the ☰ button to open it.

Time & attendance

Attendance

Record your working time so it counts towards your pay, and review your entries.

Clocking in & out

  1. On the Dashboard, press the big red Clock In button when you start. (Leave the type on Regular for a normal shift.)
  2. While working, the button turns green and shows your running worked time.
  3. Taking a break? Use Start Break or Lunch Break — the timer pauses so breaks aren't counted. Press Resume Work when you're back.
  4. Press Clock Out at the end of your shift.

On site, your site head can scan your QR code to clock you in or out instead — no typing needed.

Important Always clock out — an open entry can't be approved or paid.

On the Attendance page

Open Attendance from the sidebar to see all your time entries — date, clock in/out, total hours, and status: pending approved rejected. Use the date and status filters to find a specific entry.

End of day

My Day (End-of-day log)

Write a short summary of your day, tag the jobs you worked on, and add photos.

  1. Open My Day from the sidebar.
  2. Under What did you accomplish? type an item and press Enter to add it to the list. Add as many as you like.
  3. Add Blockers and Plan for tomorrow if relevant.
  4. Tag the Jobs worked on from the dropdown, and add Photos of your work.
  5. Press Submit EOD when your day is done (or Save draft to keep editing).
Tip Jot accomplishments down as you go — it's quicker than remembering everything at 5pm. You'll get a reminder if you haven't submitted by end of day.

Your work

My Jobs

See the jobs assigned to you and move them along as you work.

  1. Open My Jobs from the sidebar. Jobs are grouped by date.
  2. Each card shows the title, project/customer, time, van, and crew.
  3. Use the Upcoming / Past / All pills and the status/date filters to narrow the list.
  4. When a job is scheduled, press ▶ Start when you begin; when in progress, press ✓ Complete when finished.
Note Starting/completing a job is not the same as clocking in — keep both up to date.

Your week

Schedule

See the days you're rostered to work, and this week's jobs.

  1. Open Schedule from the sidebar.
  2. The Staff Roster shows your scheduled working days for the week, with shift times where set.
  3. Switch tabs to see this week's jobs alongside the roster.
  4. Use the week arrows to look ahead or back.
Note Your working pattern and shifts are set by your manager — check here so you know when you're expected in.

The month at a glance

Calendar

A monthly view of jobs, leave, and project schedules.

  1. Open Calendar from the sidebar.
  2. Each day shows jobs and leave as chips; projects appear as bars spanning their dates.
  3. Use the arrows to move between months, or press Today to jump back.
  4. The legend at the top explains what each mark means.

Extra hours

Overtime

File overtime so extra hours are approved and paid.

  1. Open Overtime from the sidebar and click Request OT.
  2. Choose the date, start and end times, and the type (OT or RDOT), then add a reason.
  3. Submit — it goes to your manager as pending for approval.
  4. Track the outcome on the same page (approved / rejected).
How it affects pay Regular pay is capped at 8 hours per shift. Anything beyond 8 hours is only paid if you file it as overtime and it's approved — otherwise the extra time isn't paid.

Time off

Leave

Request time off and track your balance.

  1. Open Leave from the sidebar. The top shows your allowance, days left, and sick days.
  2. Click Request Leave, choose the type (Annual, Sick, Compassionate, Unpaid, Other) and the dates, add a reason.
  3. Check the working-days preview (weekends aren't counted), then Submit.
  4. Your request goes to your manager: pending → approved / rejected.
Tip Request annual leave well in advance so it can be planned around jobs. Keep an eye on your Days left.

Stay connected

News Feed

The company's social wall for announcements, posts, and recognition.

  1. Open Newsfeed from the sidebar. Click What's on your mind? to write a post.
  2. Pick a type (Post, Blog, Event, Recognition), type your message, and add up to six photos.
  3. Type @ to mention a colleague, or @all to notify everyone.
  4. React and comment on posts; you can edit your own posts and comments.

Organise tasks

Workspaces & Boards

Visual boards for organising tasks — like digital sticky notes.

  1. Open Workspace from the sidebar and click a board to open it.
  2. Boards have lists (columns) and cards (tasks). Add a card with + Add a card.
  3. Drag cards between lists to move them along; click a card to open its details.
  4. Inside a card: description, labels, dates, checklist, attachments (images, files, video), and comments. Type @ to mention a teammate.

On site

My QR Code

Your personal QR code for clocking in/out on site.

  1. Open My QR Code from the sidebar (it's also on your Dashboard).
  2. Show it to your site head so they can scan you in or out — no typing needed.

Your pay

My Payslip

See your hours and pay for a period.

  1. Open My Payslip from the sidebar.
  2. It shows the period, your shift details, hours (regular and overtime), and gross pay.
  3. Use the date filter to view a specific range, or open a past payslip from the list.
Note Times are shown in your own timezone. Hours beyond 8 per shift appear only if they were approved as overtime.

Help & learning

Knowledge Base & Training

Guides and training material, any time.

  1. Open Knowledge Base from the sidebar to browse help articles by category.
  2. Use On this page on the right of an article to jump between sections.
  3. Open Training for learning material assigned to you.

Client work

Client Projects

View projects from connected client systems (BGR and BCF).

  1. Open Client Projects from the sidebar and choose BGR or BCF.
  2. The first time, connect your account — enter your BGR Portal credentials once; they're stored securely and you won't be asked again.
  3. Once connected, your assigned projects and orders appear here.
Not linked? If you see "Account not linked", ask an admin to link your account in Staff → Edit.

Your account

Your Profile

Keep your details and password up to date.

  1. Click your name/avatar at the bottom of the sidebar and choose Profile.
  2. Update your photo, name, email, hire date, emergency contact, and certifications, then Save Changes.
  3. Change your password in the Change Password section.
Managed by HR Your role, pay rate, contracted hours, and timezone are set by HR/admin — contact your manager if any look wrong.

Staying informed

Notifications

The portal keeps you in the loop automatically.

  • The bell at the top of the screen shows new notifications — a job assigned, a mention, a leave decision, a reviewed EOD log, and more.
  • Click a notification to jump straight to what it's about.
  • Some notifications are also emailed to you so you don't miss company news.

That's the whole portal. If you get stuck, check the Knowledge Base or ask your manager. Welcome aboard!
